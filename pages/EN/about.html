About Us

Founded in 1924

The VangelicoÂ® Fine Jewelry story began in 1924 when Michael de Vanta and Ben Gelico opened the first Vangelico Fine Jewelry retail store in Los Santos, California. Along with offering high quality merchandise at the lowest prices, the store offered a revolutionary credit plan of "a penny down and a dollar a week" on many higher priced items. This marketing strategy proved to be successful, and, paired with exceptional customer service provided by dedicated employees, led to the jeweler's expansion to 12 stores across other states by 1941.
